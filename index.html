PROJECTS WE'RE UP TO

Hi! It's me. Lori Wood, the creator of The Wood House. I'm a stay at home mom with a passion for interior design and refurbushing furniture so I took a leap and started this business through Instagram!

Let me help you furnish and decorate your home, whether you're starting fresh or wanting to add to what you have! I offer eDesign to those that aren't local to the Champaign, IL area.

If you have a piece of furniture that is solid, but it doesn't fit your style, I'd love to give it new life by refinishing it or giving it a fresh paint job.

Send me a message on Instagram or email me at thewoodhouse.design@gmail.com so we can set up a free consultation! We will work together to help you love your home more.
